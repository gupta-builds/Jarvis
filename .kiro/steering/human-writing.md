# Human Writing Steering

Before drafting or rewriting Markdown in this workspace:

1. Read `HUMAN_WRITING.md`.
2. Follow it as the canonical writing standard.

Non-negotiable rules:

- Do not write generic AI prose.
- Remove filler and repeated framing.
- Prefer concrete mechanisms, real examples from this vault, and explicit contrasts.
- If a sentence sounds polished but carries no real information, delete or rewrite it.
- Keep uncertainty honest.
- Leave notes denser, clearer, and more human than before.
