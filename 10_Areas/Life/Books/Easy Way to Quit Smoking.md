## Allen Carr’s Easy way to Quit
- Carr suggests reading the book, choosing the time you’re going to stop, and then going for it. Before that, you need to ask yourself what you gain from smoking. In essence, you gain absolutely nothing and you lose a lot. Smoking does nothing for you at all; it ruins your health, it makes you a social outcast and it takes away all your money. Once you begin, you’re trapped. What you need to do in order to quit successfully is come to terms with the fact that smoking brings zero benefit to your life.
