# Habits
1. First, choose one specific behavior you’re going to change starting right now. 
2. Second, commit to doing it for just three days - not forever, just three days.
3. Third, if you stumble, revise your plan, be realistic with your goals, and continue. But remember to check in with yourself to remember your aim. Finally, focus on consistency over perfection. 
4. The big question is: What is the one action for you?
5. *Make a Habit Dashboard: Good habits, bad habits, neutral habits*. These are things that you do every single day, try to be as detailed as possible.
