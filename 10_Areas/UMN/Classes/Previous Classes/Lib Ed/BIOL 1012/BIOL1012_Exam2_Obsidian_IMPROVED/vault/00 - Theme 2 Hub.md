## The Big Picture

Theme 2 tells one connected story:

1. **Blood** carries oxygen, immune cells, and nutrients through the cardiovascular system.
2. **DNA** (via the central dogma) determines which proteins — including immune antigens — cells make.
3. **Innate immunity** responds first and broadly to any pathogen.
4. **Adaptive immunity** recognizes specific antigens, builds memory, and is responsible for organ rejection.
5. **Vaccines** exploit adaptive memory to give protection before real infection.
6. **Transplantation** requires matching antigens so adaptive immunity does not reject the organ.
7. **Ethics** shapes how all of this is practiced — who gets organs, how research is done, who controls vaccines.
