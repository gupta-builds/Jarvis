## Key Points to Know

### Vaccine Components
- **Antigen:** active ingredient — weakened/inactivated pathogen, isolated proteins, or mRNA.
- **Adjuvant:** boosts innate response (e.g., aluminum salts).
- **Preservatives/Buffers:** keep vaccine stable and prevent contamination.
- **Lipids:** only in mRNA vaccines; help get mRNA into cells.

### Immune Response to Vaccine
- **Innate first:** inflammation, fever, fatigue at injection site.
- **Adaptive primary response:** encounter → activate → attack (limited, no real pathogen) → **memory cells formed**.
- On first real infection: memory cells respond → **secondary response** (fast, strong, protective).

### Herd Immunity
- **R₀:** how many people each infected person infects.
- **HIT:** vaccination % needed to stop uncontrolled spread.
- Measles: R₀ ≈ 20, HIT ≈ 95%.
- Delta COVID: R₀ = 5–7, HIT ≈ 80–87.5%.

### Key Case Facts
- **Wakefield:** fabricated data, 12 kids, no control group, funded by lawyers — every ethical pillar violated; caused measles outbreaks including Minneapolis Somali community (2017).
- **Correlation ≠ Causation:** vaccinated children also have autism at similar ages; does not mean vaccines cause autism.
- **Salk / Polio:** formaldehyde killed the virus; Salk didn't patent it (beneficence/justice).
