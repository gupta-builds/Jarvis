# Discussion
## Pre-work
1. Assignment (required to upload to dropbox as well as posted here in the discussion section) due date for assignments each week is Saturday with the answer to the classmate questions (not required to upload to dropbox, just post as a reply here in the discussion section) due date is Sunday, both at 11:30pm.  You must to submit your answer to the assignment questions to the dropbox AND the discussion section.  Don't forget to ask a question of another student, and answer another student's question for EACH assignment.
2. Minimum word count for assignments is 400 words. 
3. Turnitin similarity score on your assignments submitted to the dropbox is a max of 15%.
4. Ask a question at the end of each assignment that is relevant to your answer and the topic.
5. For responses to student questions, target about 100 words but less or more is ok as there is no actual minimum.
6. Post here, and upload to the dropbox (Assignments tab, then the correct assignment number) as well.
7. Make a good faith effort to answer the questions I post as assignments.  Always use data and details to prove your points.  Remember, you are trying to convince the reader that what you are saying is correct.  Facts are needed for that. avoid such nonsense as, "On page 43, Jones says blah blah blah"  That is really just padding the word count and is best avoided.
8.  When writing your answer, be sure to include one quote from your textbook in your answer. 
It does not need to be long, but one quote is required. All parts must be completed for credit.  There is no partial credit.  Determining credit is simple.  If you followed all the requirements in this list, you have credit.  If you did not, you don't.
