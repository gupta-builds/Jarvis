# HIST 1103 — Schedule
The official syllabus schedule below. The date shown is the **Monday** of each week. This schedule is subject to change. (Note: these "assignments due this week" dates can differ from the per-item due dates in D2L — see [[HIST 1103 - Assignments and Discussions]].)
## Week-by-week
| Week of (Mon) | Assignments due this week | Chapters to read this week |
|---|---|---|
| June 1 | Practice, 1, 2, and 3 | Intro, 1, 2, 3, and 4 |
| June 8 | 4, 5, 6, and 7 | 5, 6, 7, and 8 |
| June 15 | Midterm Exam and Assignment 8 | 9, 10, 11, 12 |
| June 22 | 9, 10, 11, and 12 | 13, 14, 15, 16 |
| June 29 | 13 and Final Exam | 17 |
## Hard deadlines
- **Final Exam:** due Thursday, **July 2, 11:30 PM** — no late finals accepted.
- **Assignment 13:** due **July 3, 11:30 PM**.
- **Midterm Exam:** due Thursday, **June 18, 11:30 PM** (questions posted Monday June 15 by noon).
## Notes
- Assignments for a week (usually 4) are posted Monday–Thursday by noon; one assignment is posted after the Midterm during the week the Midterm is assigned.
- The pace is a full 16-week course in 4 weeks — read ahead and stay in front of the deadlines.
