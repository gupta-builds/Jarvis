# Jarvis

Jarvis is the separate project for building this vault into an AI-powered second brain, research engine, learning system, and shared context layer for every LLM/tool I use.

## North Star

Jarvis should become the place where my AI tools go to understand me, my work, my concepts, my projects, my learning gaps, and my previous conversations.

The goal is not to make more AI notes. The goal is to make the vault think better:

- every important note becomes learnable
- every AI reads shared context through Obsidian/MCP before helping
- every LLM conversation can be captured, summarized, and linked
- every project can use Jarvis as its memory layer
- every answer can cite the vault and say what is missing
