# Justin Guitar
## Grade 1
1. Module 0: Basics of Guitar:
	- Posture, How to hold the guitar, Hand placement, etc.
	- Strumming without a pick
2. Module 1: How to Play A and D chords
	- D Major
	- A Major: Figure out finger placement, comes with practice. it should be 2:1:3 in the image
	- *1 Minute Exercise*: Switch between D chord to A chord within a minute and record how many times you were able to do it in a minute. Ideally 60 chords a minute. Ever second a chord change.
		- 15/1: 10 times, 
	- Strum on the beat: 1 .. 2 .. 3 .. 4 .. 1. Strum at 1 only and be able to keep the consistent despite the hand movements.
	- *Songs to Practice*: 
		1. Dance in the night away by The Maverick
		2. [You sound good to me by Lucy hale](https://tabs.ultimate-guitar.com/tab/lucy-hale/you-sound-good-to-me-chords-1449893)
		3. [Born In The Usa Chords by Bruce Springsteen](https://tabs.ultimate-guitar.com/tab/bruce-springsteen/born-in-the-usa-chords-148158)
		4. [Silence by Marshmello](https://tabs.ultimate-guitar.com/tab/marshmello/silence-chords-2106907)
	- *Daily Practice Routine*: First 3 mins play the d chord perfectly again and again, then the next 5 mins time yourself for the 1 minute exercise. Now practice moving from chord to chord doing anchor finger exercise for 5 mins. Next 10 mins practice a song.
