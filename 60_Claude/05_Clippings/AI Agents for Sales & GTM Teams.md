## Our AI adoption roadmap

Start wherever you are. Relevance grows with you and evolves as models improve. No rip and replace — ever.

L1. Assisted

![](https://cdn.relevanceai.com/images/autonomy-levels/autonomy-l1-assisted.webp)

Start by delegating busywork - research, CRM updates, drafting emails - to SuperGTM. You steer, and it accelerates.

L2. Copilot

![](https://cdn.relevanceai.com/images/autonomy-levels/autonomy-l2-copilot.webp)

Teach SuperGTM about your playbooks. It starts to own end-to-end workflows like outbound, or meeting preparation.

L3. Autopilot

![](https://cdn.relevanceai.com/images/autonomy-levels/autonomy-l3-autopilot.webp)

As playbooks solidify, turn them into AI Workforces that run autonomously, acting on pipeline signals. You handle escalations.

L4. Self-Driving

![](https://cdn.relevanceai.com/images/autonomy-levels/autonomy-l4-selfdriving.webp)

Your AI workforces optimize themselves, building new agents and running their own tests. You lead strategy.
