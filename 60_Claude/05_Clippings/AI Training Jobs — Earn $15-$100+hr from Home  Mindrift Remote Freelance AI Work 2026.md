## What we’ve built together

From uncovering vulnerabilities to refining video, see what our trainer community made possible (and learned!) this year.

- ### Creativity Project: shaping video standards
	To improve the quality of AI-generated videos for an emerging media powerhouse, a group of trainers put their analytical and creative skills to the test.
	They analyzed motion, realism, and style to flag what felt artificial and set new industry standards while exploring AI's impact on their field.
- ![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/xQfSzvzoPslyMiBh6pPTZQuBQ.png?width=800&height=800)
	### Sabina
	### QA
	I was amazed by how AI handles video generation. This project helped me see the connection between text and visuals in a completely new way, inspired by both lifelike footage and surreal content.
- ### Safety project: exposing hidden risks
	For one of the world’s largest AI companies, trainers exposed safety risks by thinking like malicious users — crafting harmful prompts, simulating attacks, and finding blind spots in systems built for global scale.
	Along the way, they sharpened their creativity, resilience, and ethical awareness while helping build safer AI.
- ![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/SFIOWLbjnKtnRrQex39SZq5u3I0.png?width=800&height=800)
	### Nicole
	### QA
	We had to remind everyone that while you may not feel this way, there are people out there that do. So unfortunately, they had to put themselves in the mindset of someone that would be malicious. It's tough, but people did it.
- ### Reasoning Project: testing real-world scenarios
	For a leading frontier-model developer, trainers built cases from medicine, finance, and education—real-world scenarios from offices, clinics, and classrooms worldwide.
	The goal? Improve safety and reliability for models used by millions. In the process, they gained deeper insights into their own fields and how AI will shape their workflows and careers.
