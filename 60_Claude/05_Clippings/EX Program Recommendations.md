### Get Support

[Learn How to Use the EX Community Read tips from EX Community members about how to use the EX Community to get support, help others on their quit journeys, and get started.](https://www.becomeanex.org/ex-resources/about-quitting/get-ready-to-quit/how-to-use-the-ex-community/)[Visit the EX Community Connect with other people quitting and experts from the Mayo Clinic Nicotine Dependence Center.](https://excommunity.becomeanex.org/)[Sign Up For Texts From EX Program Get tips and advice unique to your quitting journey with texts from EX Program.](https://www.becomeanex.org/edit-profile/)[Take the Daily Pledge Join other people pledging to stay smoke, vape, and tobacco-free for the day in a chain of accountability.](https://excommunity.becomeanex.org/t5/Celebrations/bd-p/celebrations-events)[Check Out a Live Online Event Find live online events or watch past recordings to learn from experts and people on their quitting journeys.](https://excommunity.becomeanex.org/t5/Events/bd-p/events)[Build Your Support System Identify the people who can help you quit and who you may want to avoid for a while.](https://www.becomeanex.org/build-your-support-system/)
