**What is a privacy policy?**

A legal document that explains how an organization collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data. It informs users about their privacy rights and how their information is handled.

  
**What is personal data?**

Any information that can identify a living individual, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, IP addresses, or location data.

  
**Why is a privacy policy important?**

It builds trust with your users by being transparent about data practices. It also helps you comply with legal requirements and reduces the risk of penalties or disputes.

  
**Is it safe to use a privacy policy template?**

Yes, but it must be customized to reflect your specific practices. A template provides a solid starting point, but you should review it carefully and consult a lawyer to ensure it meets your legal obligations.

  
**What are terms and conditions?**

A legally binding agreement that defines the rules for using your app or service. It covers acceptable use, user rights and responsibilities, account termination, disclaimers, dispute resolution, and governing law.
