## Your questions, answered

---

What exactly is data privacy compliance?

It’s all about how personal data (like emails, cookies, or payment info) is collected, stored, and used. Laws like GDPR and CCPA set the rules. Compliance means having the right policies, consent flows, and safeguards in place so that data is handled legally and your business remains protected.

Which laws do iubenda solutions cover?

We cover all major global privacy laws, like the EU/UK GDPR, EU ePrivacy (Cookie Law), Brazil’s LGPD, Switzerland’s FADP, and US state laws.

How secure are iubenda’s solutions?

Very. Everything is hosted securely, with strong encryption and access controls, and aligned with standards like ISO 27001.

Can iubenda match my brand style?

Yes. You can customize our tools to fit your site design, or even white-label them if you prefer.

Is iubenda just for small businesses?

Not at all. Startups, SMEs, and big enterprises all use iubenda. Plans scale with you, so you’ll always have the right fit.
