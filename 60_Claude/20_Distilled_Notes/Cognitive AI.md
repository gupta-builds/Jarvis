# Cognitive AI

## Definition

AI systems that understand how the human brain processes reality — not just processing language or generating images, but predicting and modeling cognitive states like attention, engagement, and comprehension.

## Key Characteristics

- Goes beyond AGI — focused on understanding cognition itself, not general intelligence
- Uses multimodal inputs (video, audio, text) to predict brain activity
- Enables real-time feedback on cognitive states

## Current State

**TRIBE v2** (Meta, 2026) is the foundational model:
- Predicts whole-brain activity across 70,000 voxels
- 70x resolution increase over prior approaches
- Zero-shot: works on new people without calibration
- Open-source on Hugging Face

## Applications

| Domain | Application |
|--------|-------------|
| Wearables | Devices that understand attention/engagement in real-time |
| Education | Content adapts to predicted brain engagement |
| Mental Health | Diagnostics via passive observation |
| Research | In-silico neuroscience replaces expensive fMRI |
| Content | Optimize for understanding, not just clicks |
