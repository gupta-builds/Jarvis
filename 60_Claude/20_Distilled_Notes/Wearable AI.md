# Wearable AI

## Definition

AI-powered wearable devices that capture sensory input (what you see, hear) and understand cognitive processing (how your brain responds) in real-time.

## Distinction from Current Wearables

| Current Wearables | Wearable AI |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Record data (steps, heart rate) | Understand cognitive states |
| Passive logging | Real-time adaptation |
| Quantified self | Understood self |

## Technical Foundation

**TRIBE v2** enables this category by:
- Predicting brain activity from video, audio, and text
- Working zero-shot on new users (no calibration needed)
- Running at 70,000 voxel resolution

## Applications

1. **Education** — Glasses that detect when you're confused and adjust explanation
2. **Therapy** — Continuous mental health monitoring via cognitive patterns
3. **Productivity** — Devices that know your peak focus states
4. **Content Creation** — Test how audiences will process your work before publishing

## Market Projection

- **2030 TAM:** $340B (largest of cognitive AI applications)
- **Timeline:** Consumer devices within 18 months
